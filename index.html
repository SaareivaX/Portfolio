About

A little about me.

Hi there! I'm Xaviera Ananda Sinaiyangsih, a communication science student at Universitas Nasional, Indonesia, with a passion for public relations. I'm intrigued by the power of communication to connect people, shape narratives, and spark positive change. Whether it's crafting compelling content, building strategic campaigns, or fostering understanding between diverse audiences, I'm always eager to learn and contribute my unique perspective.

As a student at Universitas Nasional, I find myself constantly stimulated by the dynamic and intellectually invigorating environment. Here, I've built a strong foundation in communication theories, media studies, and strategic communication, gaining valuable knowledge that empowers my understanding of the field. But learning for me extends beyond lectures and textbooks. I actively engage in extracurricular activities, refining my practical skills in public speaking, journalism, and digital media production. This hands-on experience allows me to bridge the gap between theory and practice, honing my ability to communicate effectively across diverse platforms and situations.
